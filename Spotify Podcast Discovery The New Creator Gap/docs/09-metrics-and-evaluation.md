# Chapter 9: Success Metrics & Evaluation

This chapter outlines the quantitative frameworks used to evaluate product performance across both ends of the Spotify podcast marketplace.

---

## Success Metrics Framework

To measure the health of both the creator supply-side and the listener demand-side, we establish a tiered metrics framework:

| Metric Type | Metric Name | Definition & Formula | Strategic Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Leading** | Creator Dashboard WAU | Weekly Active Users of the Discovery Health portal / Total registered creators <90 days | Measures creator engagement with recommendation feedback. |
| **Leading** | "New Voices" Shelf CTR | Click-Through Rate on the newly added home screen shelf cards | Measures listener willingness to try newly surfaced creators. |
| **Leading** | Average Listen Completion Rate (LCR) | Consumed duration / Total episode duration (averaged over first 3 episodes) | Evaluates if the quality gate is filtering out low-quality audio content. |
| **Lagging** | 90-Day Creator Retention Rate | % of creators who publish ≥5 episodes within their first 90 days | Primary indicator of supply-side health and reduction in podfading. |
| **Lagging** | Cohort Growth Rate | % of shows reaching 100+ unique listeners in 90 days | Validates whether the algorithmic surface translates to audience formation. |
| **Lagging** | Podcast recommendation CSAT | Customer Satisfaction Score isolated for the "Recommended for You" section | Ensures catalog diversification does not hurt listener rating profiles. |
| **Guardrail** | Home Feed Interaction Rate | Total home screen clicks / Total sessions | Confirms the new shelf does not cannibalize premium content interactions. |
| **Guardrail** | Session Duration | Average listening time per user session | Ensures that surfacing unproven creators does not drive down overall time-on-app. |
